## Audio & Visual Questions

### What are binaural beats?

Binaural beats are an auditory illusion created when two slightly different frequencies are played in each ear. Your brain perceives a third tone (the difference between the two frequencies), which can influence brainwave states.

### Are the visuals based on real cosmic phenomena?

## Troubleshooting

### The app won't start

1. Ensure your OS is up to date
2. Restart your device
3. Delete and reinstall the app
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### No audio is playing

1. Check system volume
2. Verify audio output device
3. Grant audio permissions in System Settings
4. Try headphones to isolate speaker issues

### Visuals are stuttering

1. Close other intensive applications
2. Reduce screen resolution temporarily
3. Restart the application
4. Update to the latest version

For more troubleshooting help, see [[Troubleshooting]] or contact [support@universalmanifestor.app](mailto:support@universalmanifestor.app).
