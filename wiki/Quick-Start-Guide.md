# Quick Start Guide

Get started with Universal Manifestor in just 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Download & Install

1. Download Universal Manifestor from the [App Store](#) (link pending)
2. Install the application on your device
3. Launch the app

## Step 2: Choose Your Mode

Universal Manifestor offers four distinct modes:

### 🎯 Focus Mode
**Best for:** Starting your day, pre-work preparation, mental clarity

**What it does:** Sharpens your attention and reduces mental scatter

**Duration:** 5-15 minutes recommended

### 🌀 Align Mode  
**Best for:** Restoring balance, stress relief, emotional regulation

**What it does:** Harmonizes your energy centers and restores equilibrium

**Duration:** 10-20 minutes recommended

### ✨ Manifest Mode
**Best for:** Setting intentions, goal achievement, desire manifestation

**What it does:** Focuses your consciousness on specific outcomes

**Duration:** 15-30 minutes recommended

### 🌌 Universe Mode
**Best for:** Deep meditation, spiritual connection, expanded awareness

**What it does:** Connects you with infinite universal possibilities

**Duration:** 20-45 minutes recommended

## Step 3: Set Your Intention

Before beginning your session:

1. **Find a quiet space** where you won't be disturbed
2. **Sit or lie comfortably** 
3. **Take three deep breaths** to center yourself
4. **Clearly state your intention** (silently or aloud)

Example intentions:
- "I intend to release stress and find inner peace"
- "I intend to manifest clarity in my career path"
- "I intend to align with my highest good"

## Step 4: Begin Your Session

1. Click on your chosen mode
2. Allow the visualization to load
3. Let the soundscape wash over you
4. Follow any guided prompts
5. Stay present with the experience

## Step 5: Complete & Integrate

When your session ends:

1. Take a moment to notice how you feel
2. Take three conscious breaths
3. Set an intention to carry this energy into your day
4. Slowly return to normal activity

## Tips for Success

✅ **Consistency**: Practice at the same time daily for best results  
✅ **Environment**: Create a dedicated practice space  
✅ **Openness**: Approach with curiosity and without expectation  
✅ **Patience**: Benefits compound over time  
✅ **Integration**: Take inspired action after sessions  

## Common First-Time Questions

**Q: What should I do during the session?**  
A: Simply be present. Watch the visuals, listen to the sounds, and let thoughts come and go without attachment.

**Q: Can I fall asleep during a session?**  
A: Yes! Especially with Universe mode. Many users find it helpful for sleep.

**Q: How often should I practice?**  
A: Daily practice yields the best results. Even 5-10 minutes daily is more effective than occasional long sessions.

**Q: What if I don't feel anything?**  
A: Effects can be subtle at first. Continue practicing daily and notice shifts over days and weeks.
