Common Issues & Solutions

App won't start or crashes on launch
  • Ensure you're running macOS 13.0+ or iOS 16.0+
  • Restart your device completely
  • Try deleting and reinstalling the app
  • Check if macOS/iOS needs an update
No audio is playing
  • Check your system volume is not muted
  • Verify the correct audio output device is selected
  • Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone & Audio and ensure Universal Manifestor has permission
  • Try headphones to isolate speaker issues
Visual glitches or stuttering
  • Close other intensive applications
  • Reduce screen resolution temporarily
  • Restart the application
  • Update to the latest version of Universal Manifestor
Practice data not syncing
  • Ensure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices
  • Check iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings
  • Try toggling iCloud sync off and on
  • Restart all devices
Can't download from App Store
  • Verify your device meets minimum requirements
  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Try signing out and back into your Apple ID

System Requirements

macOS

  • macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later
  • Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) or Intel
  • 2GB RAM minimum
  • 500MB available storage

iOS/iPadOS

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch
  • 1GB RAM recommended
  • 500MB available storage

Before You Contact Support

To help us assist you better, please include:

  • Your device model (e.g., MacBook Pro M2, iPhone 15 Pro)
  • Operating system version (e.g., macOS 14.2, iOS 17.2)
  • Universal Manifestor version (found in Settings → About)
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if applicable
  • Any error messages you're seeing
